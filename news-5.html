Invitation to participate in the XIX Balkan Mineral Processing Congress – BMPC 2023

20.01.2023

The Scientific Committee of the Balkan Mineral Beneficiation Congress and the Organizing Committee of the 19th Balkan Mineral Beneficiation Congress cordially invite scientists, research scholars, specialists and managers of mineral beneficiation from the entire Balkan region, Europe and beyond to participate in the Congress: “Mineral enrichment in the global challenge”. The Congress will be held from May 28-31, 2023, in the city of Mitrovica, Republic of Kosovo (www.bmpc-2023.net). “Isa Boletini” University of Mitrovica – Faculty of Geosciences in cooperation with the Institute of Minerals and Waste, Energy and Environment (IGJPMMEM) will organize the 19th Balkan Minerals Beneficiation Congress. This Congress is held for the first time in the Republic of Kosovo.

Congress participants will present scientific papers, establish contacts with new and reputable colleagues, share experiences on innovative technologies of beneficiation of useful minerals, application of results from scientific research in industry and the role played by Mineral Beneficiation in the economy of the Balkans and other countries in the world.

The Congress also provides opportunities for mining and processing companies to present and promote methodologies, exploitation of mineral resources and new technologies of mineral treatment and enrichment.
